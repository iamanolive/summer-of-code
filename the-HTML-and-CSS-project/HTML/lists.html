Classic cheesecake

cheesecake image goes here

Ingredients

  • Four 8-ounce blocks of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients
  2. Add sugar and vanilla extract
  3. Cool it in the fridge
  4. Enjoy!
